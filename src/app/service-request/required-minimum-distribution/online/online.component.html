Notice of Tax Withholding Election
If no tax withholding election was selected, we will withhold 10% of the taxable portion of any payment for federal income taxes. State income tax may also be required to be withheld. If you elect not to have withholding apply or if you do not have enough federal income tax withheld, you may be responsible for payment of estimated tax. You may incur penalties under the estimated tax rules if your withholding and estimated tax payments are not sufficient. You may wish to consult your tax advisor regarding your tax situation.
